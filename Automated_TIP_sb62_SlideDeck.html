TIP Objectives & Paris Agreement Context

Strengthening Technology Support for Developing Countries

  • Recalling Article 10 of the Paris Agreement on technology.
  • Reaffirming technology's role in adaptation and mitigation.
  • Strengthening support for technology priorities in developing countries.
  • Addressing challenges identified in previous Technology Mechanism assessments.

Technology Priorities & Implementation

Country-Driven Approaches & Financial Support

  • Priorities include new/emerging technologies (CDR, clean hydrogen, AI).
  • Emphasis on country-driven approaches.
  • Mobilizing syndicated and blended finance (bilateral, multilateral, carbon markets).
  • Role of Article 6.8 (Non-Market Approaches) for adaptation.

Review & Prioritization of Indicators

Ensuring Measurability & Data Availability

  • Experts reviewed indicators based on CMA 6 and SB 60 criteria.
  • Focus on measurability, data availability, and relevance.
  • Consideration of traditional knowledge and disaggregation.
  • Need for better indicator structure and content.

Challenges & Gaps Identified

Bridging Policy and Implementation Divides

  • Underestimation of forest disturbances in models.
  • Disconnect between high-level policy and ground-level implementation.
  • Inconsistent disaggregation by demographic/socioeconomic factors.
  • Data gaps and methodological inconsistencies.
  • Limited exploration of MOI indicators due to time constraints.

Suggestions for Way Forward

Enhancing Collaboration & Refinement

  • More collaboration across targets for cross-cutting indicators.
  • Refining selected indicators and clarifying metadata.
  • Establishing online database for metadata and data sources.
  • Opportunities for wider stakeholder review.
  • Dynamic, inclusive process for updates based on scientific/technical progress.

Driving Technology for Climate Action

  • Technology development and transfer are central to Paris Agreement goals.
  • Proactive, country-driven approaches are essential for effective implementation.
  • Continued collaboration and resource mobilization are key to success.
